Plan Your Adventure: Boat & Daily Van Tours

Explore the Karaburun-Sazan National Marine Park

Embark on a guided boat tour of the breathtaking Karaburun-Sazan National Marine Park. Experience crystal-clear waters, hidden caves, and pristine beaches. For an added thrill, include a speedboat excursion to the famous Haxhi Ali Cave. Choose from our three unique tour options:

The Ultimate Party Cruise

A lively tour with guided exploration, beach stops, and onboard entertainment, featuring a DJ and a vibrant atmosphere.

The Adventure Voyage

A family-friendly journey with guided exploration, beach stops, and onboard entertainment with a pirate & monkey show.

The Serenity Escape

A peaceful, relaxing tour for those seeking a quiet experience with guided exploration and tranquil beach stops.

Explore Albania’s Southern Gems with Our Daily Van Tours

Explore the beauty of Albania’s southern coast with a visit to the Blue Eye spring, the lively city of Saranda, and the picturesque beaches of Ksamil. Or, immerse yourself in the history of Berat, a UNESCO city renowned for its Ottoman architecture and rich heritage.

Saranda & Ksamil Escape

Explore the southern coast from Vlora, visiting the Blue Eye spring, Saranda, and the white-sand beaches of Ksamil, with time to relax and enjoy local dining.

Berat Heritage Tour

Discover Berat, a UNESCO city renowned for its Ottoman architecture, historic streets, and Berat Castle, offering a rich cultural experience on a day trip from Vlora.

Vlora City Tour

Experience Vlora’s rich heritage with visits to Zvernec Monastery, Kanina Castle, and the Ancient City of Orikum, paired with scenic coastal views and a relaxing beach stop.
